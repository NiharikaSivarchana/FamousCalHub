Blackpink&Dualipa collab

"Kiss and Make Up" is a song by English singer Dua Lipa and South Korean girl group Blackpink from Dua Lipa: Complete Edition (2018), the super deluxe reissue of the former's eponymous debut studio album. The song was written by Lipa, Chelcee Grimes, Yannick Rastogi, Zacharie Raymond, Mathieu Jomphe-Lepine, Marc Vincent, and Teddy Park, while production was handled by Banx & Ranx. It was released through Warner Bros. Records as the second promotional single from the reissue on 19 October 2018, alongside the reissue's release. "Kiss and Make Up" is a dance, electropop, and reggaeton track, with tropical and EDM elements. Lyrically, the song is about using physical affection instead of words to solve an argument between two lovers..

"On the Ground" is the debut single by Korean-New Zealand singer and Blackpink member Rosé. It was released on 12 March 2021 by YG Entertainment as the lead single from her debut single album, R. It was written by Rosé alongside Amy Allen, Raúl Cubina, Jon Bellion, Jorgen Odegard and Teddy Park, and was produced by the latter three alongside Ojivolta and 24. The song is an electropop, pop rock and pop track with synth-pop elements.
